What Makes a "Whimsical/Cozy" Theme Work

All 4 whimsical options (3-6) share these core principles:

Visual Characteristics

  • Muted saturation - Not bright/harsh, easier on eyes
  • Warm undertones - Even cool colors feel approachable
  • Natural inspiration - Sky, earth, water, plants
  • Soft contrasts - Gentle transitions, not jarring

Emotional Impact

  • Reduces anxiety - Finances don't feel scary
  • Feels personal - Not corporate or institutional
  • Invites exploration - Users want to spend time here
  • Memorable brand - Stands out from competitors
